## 🔧 Core Features

### 🧳 Self-Booking Portal
Enable corporate clients to manage and automate business travel programs efficiently.

### 💰 Automated BSP Reconciliation
Streamline reconciliation with real-time transaction tracking and summaries.

### 🎯 Enhanced Cross-Selling
Consolidated vendor management and ancillaries sales through a unified platform.

### 🎟️ Promo Code Management
Configure offers and automatically apply relevant discounts at checkout.

### 📈 Revenue Maximization

Custom reports for expense management, supplier performance, and sales tracking.
